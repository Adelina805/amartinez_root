Who is Adelina Martinez?

Hello! I'm Adelina Martinez , a student, designer, and developer based in San Diego, CA. I like to make things for the internet. When I'm not studying, coding, or designing, you can usually find me at the beach playing volleyball, reading, buying too many houseplants, or pondering. Welcome to my corner of the internet, I made it myself :)
